Phylo4j is a graph database of biological information built on top of bio4j. In addition to the information in bio4j, BPG have added trees and orthology relationships from PhyloFacts.

Enter a UniProt accession to query phylo4j for protein protein interactions.

Enter a UniProt accession, BPG accession, etc to explore a region of the phylo4j graph.

Phylo4j development is funded by the following grant:

Funding Agency: National Science Foundation

Division: Division of Molecular and Cellular Biosciences

Title: EAGER: Towards a self-organizing map and hyper-dimensional information network for the human genome

Grant number: 1355632

Project duration: 2013-2015

PI: Kimmen Sjölander
