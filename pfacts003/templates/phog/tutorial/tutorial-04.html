You can specify a query protein sequence with its ID/accession or in FASTA sequence format.

The query protein sequence can be specified using certain allowed protein IDs/accessions or by entering a sequence in FASTA format.

[1] To see which ID/accession types are currently allowed, hover your mouse cursor over the link adjacent to the ID/accession entry box, or click here.

[2] To learn about the FASTA format, click the associated link next to the FASTA format entry box, or click here.

Later in the tutorial, we say more about retrieving a FASTA sequence in the event your sequence ID/accession is not an allowed type.
