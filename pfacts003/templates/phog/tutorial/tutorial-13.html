When you do not have an allowed ID/accession.

In the case that you do not have an allowed ID/accession for your query sequence, you can either search for an allowed ID/accession for the sequence, or you can enter the sequence itself (as described later) in FASTA format.

We will use GenBank accession DAA01234 for our example.
