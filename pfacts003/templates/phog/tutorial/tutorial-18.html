Entering a protein sequence in FASTA format.

To enter a query using a FASTA formatted protein sequence, type or paste it into the FASTA format entry box. You can also find an example FASTA formatted sequence by clicking the "FASTA format" link.

Click the "Search" button adjacent to the FASTA sequence entry box to initiate the BLAST search for matching proteins.
